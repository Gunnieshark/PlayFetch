About the Game

PlayFetch is a fun, kid-friendly game where you help Gunner, the dog catch his favourite toy, tennis balls! Avoid catching Ferris, the cat and watch out for Letty the Rottweiler as you progress through increasingly challenging levels.

How to Play

Tap and drag to move Gunner around the screen. Catch tennis balls to score points and advance levels. Avoid Ferris the cat (subtracts points) and Letty the Rottweiler (game over!).
